RIP Steve Jobs

When I started to read the news this morning, I couldn’t believe it. It felt very surreal. Not completely awake yet, I opened up other news sites to make sure it wasn’t a mistake.

I’m not sure how I feel. Mostly, I feel love. I am impressed by the products Apple made and the inspiration they gave everybody. If there’s one company that I love, it must be Apple. And loving Apple implies loving Steve Jobs.

As Marco said, I don’t feel at all qualified to write this, but it feels inappropriate to do something else.

Thank you for everything.
